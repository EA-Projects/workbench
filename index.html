For the past decade, NYC has been cultivating a world-class enterprise tech ecosystem. Thanks to a dense population of Fortune 500 buyers, tech talent, and investors, NYC is now a thriving community of enterprise companies. At Work-Bench, we have been tracking this growth for the past several years in our State of Enterprise Tech in NYC: Funding Reports, and are excited to see the fundings, exit activity, and size of the local NYC enterprise startup pipeline continue to boom.

Introducing the New York City Enterprise Tech Index

This ranking recognizes NYC’s leading enterprise software companies building disruptive and innovative new solutions for the Fortune 500’s most challenging problems. Members of the NYC Enterprise Tech Index represent the largest NYC-based private, venture-backed enterprise companies, ranked by total funding amount.

For the past decade, NYC has cultivated a world-class enterprise tech ecosystem. Thanks to a dense population of Fortune 500 buyers, tech talent, and investors, NYC is now a thriving community of enterprise companies. At Work-Bench, we have been tracking this growth for the past several years in our State of Enterprise Tech in NYC: Funding Reports, and are excited to see the fundings, exit activity, and the size of the local NYC enterprise startup pipeline continue to boom.
